Who is Eligible for Work Visa?

Educational Qualifications

  • Bachelor's Degree (minimum for most positions)
  • Professional Certifications
  • Specialized Training & Skills
  • Industry-Specific Credentials

Professional Experience

  • Minimum 2-5 years work experience
  • Relevant Industry Background
  • Proven Track Record
  • Reference Letters

Employment Requirements

  • Valid Job Offer from Employer
  • Labor Market Certification
  • Proof of Salary Package
  • Employment Contract

Documentation

  • Valid Passport
  • Job Offer Letter
  • Educational Certificates
  • Police Clearance Certificate

Types of Work Visas Available

Skilled Worker Visa (H-1B, UK Visa, etc.)

For professionals in specialized fields requiring specific expertise

  • Duration: 3-6 years
  • Sponsorship required
  • Path to permanent residency

Intra-Company Transfer Visa

For employees transferring within multinational companies

  • Duration: 2-4 years
  • Company sponsorship
  • Faster processing

Start-up/Entrepreneur Visa

For business owners and entrepreneurs

  • Duration: 2-3 years (renewable)
  • Business plan required
  • Investment criteria applies

Freelance/Contractor Visa

For self-employed professionals and contractors

  • Duration: 1-2 years
  • Self-sponsorship
  • Proof of income required

Work Visa Application Process

1

Secure Job Offer

Get a job offer from a company willing to sponsor your work visa

2

Employer Sponsorship

Employer initiates sponsorship and files required applications

3

Labor Certification

Obtain labor market certification (if required by destination country)

4

Prepare Documents

Gather credentials, certificates, employment history, and financial documents

5

Submit Application

Submit complete visa application through official immigration channels

6

Medical & Police Check

Complete medical examination and police clearance certificate

7

Visa Interview

Attend visa interview at embassy/consulate if required

8

Receive Visa & Travel

Collect your work visa and prepare for relocation

Important Points to Know

Processing Time

6-12 weeks depending on country and complexity

Visa Fees

Typically $150-$500, costs may be covered by employer

Validity Period

Usually valid for 3-5 years, renewable based on employment

Job Restriction

Usually limited to sponsored employer; changing jobs requires new sponsorship
