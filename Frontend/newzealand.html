Study in New Zealand

Why Study in New Zealand

New Zealand offers world-class education, stunning natural beauty, and excellent work-study opportunities for international students.

World-Class Education

Innovative teaching, practical learning, and globally recognised qualifications.

Work While You Study

International students can work up to 20 hours/week during studies and full-time during breaks.

Quality of Life

Safe environment, beautiful natural landscapes, and vibrant multicultural communities.

Post-Study Work Visa

Pathway to residence with up to 3 years post-study work rights after graduation.

Affordable Living

Reasonable tuition fees and living costs compared to other English-speaking countries.

Top Universities in New Zealand

University of Auckland

Auckland, North Island

New Zealand's highest-ranked university. Located in the country's largest city with diverse opportunities.

Engineering Business Medicine Law

University of Otago

Dunedin, South Island

Oldest university in New Zealand. Leading institution for health sciences and research.

Medicine Dentistry Health Sciences Sciences

Victoria University of Wellington

Wellington, North Island

Located in the capital city. Strong in law, public policy, and creative arts.

Law Public Policy Creative Arts Business

University of Canterbury

Christchurch, South Island

Strong engineering programs and research facilities. Beautiful campus in garden city.

Engineering Sciences Forestry Business

Massey University

Palmerston North, North Island

Largest university by number of students. Excellence in agriculture and veterinary sciences.

Agriculture Veterinary Science Business Aviation

Lincoln University

Canterbury, South Island

Specialist land-based university. Leading programs in agriculture and environmental management.

Agriculture Environmental Management Business Tourism
