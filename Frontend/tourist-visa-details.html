Who is Eligible for Tourist Visa?

Basic Requirements

  • Valid Passport (6+ months validity)
  • No age restrictions
  • No specific education requirements
  • Applicable to all nationalities

Financial Proof

  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Bank statements (3-6 months)
  • Income documentation
  • Travel sponsorship (optional)

Documentation

  • Valid Passport Copy
  • Travel Itinerary
  • Hotel Bookings or Accommodation Proof
  • Return Flight Ticket

Personal Criteria

  • Good Health
  • No Criminal History
  • Genuine Intent to Return
  • Stable Residence in Home Country

Tourist Visa Processing & Benefits

Processing Information

  • Duration: Typically 15 days - 2 months
  • Stay Period: 30 days to 5 years (varies by country)
  • Visa Fee: $50 - $300
  • Extension: Limited, only in special cases
  • Rejection Rate: Low for genuine tourists

Key Benefits

  • Easy and quick approval process
  • No employment restrictions for leisure
  • Multiple entry options available
  • Flexible travel dates
  • Good first-time visa option

Important Points to Know

No Employment

Tourist visas do not permit any form of paid work

No Business Activities

Cannot conduct business meetings or sign contracts

No Studies

Cannot enroll in educational institutions

Easy to Extend

Can be extended in some cases if circumstances allow
