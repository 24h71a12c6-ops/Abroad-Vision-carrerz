Who is Eligible for Student Visa?

Academic Requirements

  • High School Diploma or Equivalent
  • Minimum GPA: 2.5 - 3.0 (varies by country)
  • English Language Proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Standardized Test Scores (SAT/ACT for USA)

Financial Requirements

  • Proof of Financial Support
  • Bank Statements (min. 1-2 years)
  • Sponsor Declaration (parents/guardians)
  • Sufficient funds for tuition & living costs

Documentation

  • Valid Passport (min. 6 months validity)
  • Acceptance Letter from Institution
  • Academic Records & Certificates
  • Birth Certificate & Police Clearance

Personal Criteria

  • Good Health & Medical Clearance
  • No Criminal Record
  • Strong Intent to Study
  • Plan to Return Home (for some countries)

1

Access to Quality Education

Study at world-class universities with excellent academic standards and research opportunities

2

Work Experience

Gain practical experience through part-time work and internships (typically 15-20 hours/week during study period)

3

Post-Study Opportunities

Access to work visas and permanent residency pathways after completing your studies

4

Global Networking

Build international connections and expand your professional network worldwide

5

Travel Freedom

Travel within and outside the country with proper documentation and permissions

6

Career Advancement

Gain internationally recognized qualifications that enhance career prospects globally

Student Visa Application Process

1

Select Institution & Program

Research and apply to universities that match your academic goals and interests

2

Get Acceptance Letter

Receive official admission/acceptance letter from the educational institution

3

Arrange Finances

Secure financial support and prepare bank statements and sponsor documents

4

Prepare Documentation

Gather all required documents including passport, certificates, and medical reports

5

Submit Application

Submit your complete visa application through official channels

6

Attend Interview

Appear for visa interview at the embassy/consulate if required

7

Receive Visa

Collect your stamped passport with student visa approval

8

Arrange Travel

Book flights and prepare for your journey to study abroad

Important Points to Know

Processing Time

Typically 4-8 weeks, but can vary by country and institution

Visa Fees

Non-refundable fees ranging from $100-$300 depending on destination

Validity Period

Valid for duration of studies plus grace period (typically 30-90 days)

Extension Options

Can be extended for continuing education or further studies
